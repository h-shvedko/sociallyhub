Executive Summary

SociallyHub is an enterprise-grade social media management platform designed to streamline your social media operations, enhance client relationships, and deliver measurable results. Built for agencies, marketing teams, and businesses managing multiple social accounts, SociallyHub combines powerful automation, real-time analytics, and comprehensive client management into one unified platform.

Manage all your social media accounts from a single, intuitive dashboard:

  • Supported Platforms: Twitter, Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, YouTube, TikTok
  • Unified Inbox: Centralized hub for all social interactions across platforms
  • Content Calendar: Visual calendar for planning and scheduling posts across multiple accounts
  • Multi-Account Management: Handle unlimited social accounts with workspace isolation
  • Real-Time Monitoring: Track mentions, comments, and engagement as they happen

Scale your team efficiently with advanced role management:

Benefits:
